About

Strategy and care, applied to sustainability work

Rikke Lundsgaard works at the intersection of sustainability, organic transformation, events, and project coordination. Through RVL-CPH, she helps organizations create meaningful progress with clarity, structure, and care.

Her approach is practical and grounded: understand the ambition, map the realities, and build a path that people and partners can actually follow. The result is sustainability work that holds up — in planning, on the ground, and after the event is over.
